1. System Overview

The Delivery Orders Application (DoApp) is an internal web-based system used by Al Wahdania General Trading Co. LLC, Fujairah Branch, to manage the complete lifecycle of delivery orders — from creation through to final delivery confirmation.

1.1 User Roles & Permissions

Role Badge Key Permissions
Admin Admin Full access — create/edit/delete orders, manage products, customers, users, modules, workflow. Can perform all transitions.
Creator (Sales) Creator Create & edit draft orders, submit orders, add line items, upload delivery documents (mark as DELIVERED).
Finance Finance Review submitted orders: approve (PRICE AGREED), reject, or request attachments (NEED ATTACHMENT).
Logistics Logistics Confirm price-agreed orders, upload customs documents (CUSTOMS DOCUMENT UPDATED).

18. FAQ & Troubleshooting

Q: I cannot log in. What should I do?

Ensure you are using your Active Directory username (not email). If you are locked out, contact IT Support.

Q: I submitted an order but need to make changes. How?

You can use "Revert to Draft" if available, or ask the Finance team to reject the order. Rejected orders return to DRAFT status for editing.

Q: Why can't I see the "Price Agreed" or "Confirm" buttons?

These buttons are role-specific. Only Finance users see "Price Agreed" on SUBMITTED orders. Only Logistics users see "Confirm" on PRICE AGREED orders. Admin users can see all buttons.

Q: My file upload failed. What file types are supported?

Supported: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, JPG, JPEG, PNG, TXT, ZIP, RAR. Files are stored in the database for security.

Q: How do I upload customs documents?

Navigate to the CONFIRMED order, scroll to "Fujairah Logistics Team" section, click Edit, fill in the fields, attach files, and click "Save & Upload Customs Documents".

Q: How do I mark an order as delivered?

Navigate to the CUSTOMS DOCUMENT UPDATED order (as Creator/Sales), scroll to "Sales Team" section, click Edit, fill in delivery details, attach files, and click "Save & Upload Delivery Documents".

Q: The order shows "NEED ATTACHMENT" status. What does this mean?

Finance has requested additional documents. Upload the required files using the Attachments section, then contact Finance to proceed.

Q: Can I delete a delivery order?

No. Orders cannot be deleted. You can cancel a DRAFT order (changes status to CANCELLED) or reject a submitted order.

Q: How is the PO Number generated?

Format: AWTFZC/Mon/YY/DO#### — auto-generated based on branch code, month, year, and sequential number.
