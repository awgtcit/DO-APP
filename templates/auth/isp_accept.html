Information Security Policy

You must accept the policy before accessing the system.

1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for protecting the organization's information assets. All employees are required to comply with the guidelines outlined herein to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of organizational data.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users who have access to the organization's information systems and data.

3. Responsibilities

Users must protect login credentials, report security incidents immediately, use systems only for authorized purposes, and comply with data classification standards.

4. Acceptable Use

Company systems and networks are to be used for business purposes. Personal use should be limited and must not compromise security or productivity.

5. Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.
